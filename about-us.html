About Us



We have partnered from your favourite tiffin vendors available around you where you can manage your subscription on a click.
TiffyBro is a privately registered brand under Hungersoft Technologies Private Limited.
The main focus of the TiffyBro app/website is to provide best experience with your local Tiffin partners available near you.

We are continuously working hard from the backend to improve our service and for your better experience.
Currently we are a small team trying to change the way how you used to order & manage the tiffin services available around you.

At TiffyBro we believe that there should be a systematic way of ordering or subscribing your daily meal which we call as Tiffin, as there are numerous reasons why you can't order everyday from the restaurants.
So, to remove one single and important burden of your daily busy life we have got your back with our TiffyBro app.

As we start to get the positive response from the customers/vendors we'll definitely have something more here in this (About Us) page



Benifits



✔ Get the list and choose Different Meals from Tiffin Partners which are available in your area.

✔ Place an order for your monthly/weekly/customised Tiffin plans according to your requirements.
